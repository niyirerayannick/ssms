What this does

  • Promotes `P1` to `P2`, `P6` to `S1`, and `N3` to `P1`.
  • Marks `S6` students as graduated instead of copying them forward.
  • Creates or updates yearly enrollment snapshots without touching marks or fees.

Before you run it

  • Make sure the source academic year already has enrollment history records.
  • Review class names if you use custom formats outside the standard `P`, `S`, or `N` levels.
  • Run once first without overwrite unless you intentionally want to refresh target-year records.
